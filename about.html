Victoria Cagley is a current student at the University of New Haven. She studies Communications and Media with a concentration in Digital Media. During her time at the university, she has written for the school newspaper, the Charger Bulletin, and currently holds the position as Community Engagement Editor. She also works at the campus radio station, WNHU, as a Public Affairs Host. Victoria loves to write and work with anything online, as she see that it is the future for how our society operates.
